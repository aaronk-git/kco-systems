### Hero

**Primary CTA**: Request a Sample for Your Territory

### The Problem
**Headline**: You're finding jobs too late.

Permit data is public. But pulling it, filtering it, and turning it into something useful takes hours you don't have.

Most operators find new projects through word of mouth, driving around, or waiting for the phone to ring. By the time you hear about a job, someone else already called.

Raw permit feeds aren't much better. Hundreds of rows. No filtering. No priority. No way to tell which jobs are worth your time.

### What You Get
**Headline**: A filtered list, delivered weekly.

Every Monday, you get a curated list of construction permits for your territory — filtered for the project types that matter to your business and sorted so the highest-value opportunities are at the top.

**Each entry includes:**
- Permit date and address
- Project type (demolition, commercial remodel, new construction, major renovation)
- Contractor or builder name
- Phone number from the permit application
- Square footage

**What's excluded:**
Production builders with locked-in vendor relationships. Small jobs unlikely to need your services. Duplicate entries. Noise.

### How It's Filtered
**Headline**: Not a data dump. A filtered feed.

We pull permits from public records, then apply filters designed to surface the opportunities most relevant to your business:

- **Demolitions** — guaranteed demand, project-specific, first to call wins
- **Commercial projects** — larger scope, longer timelines, heavier service needs
- **Large custom builds** — builders who shop per-project, not locked into vendor contracts
- **Major renovations** — gut jobs and large additions that generate serious volume

The list is sorted so the biggest, most actionable opportunities are at the top.

### Limited Distribution
**Headline**: Your territory. Not everyone's.

This isn't a mass-market data feed. We limit distribution by territory — one operator per service type per area. The filtered list you receive is not shared with your direct competitors in the same zip codes.

### Who This Is For
**Good fit:**
- Dumpster / roll-off operators
- Portable sanitation providers
- Temporary fencing companies
- Site services operators
- Any business that needs to be first to new construction job sites

**Not a fit:**
- National chains with internal data teams
- Companies looking for a self-serve software platform (this is a delivered product, not a login)
- Anyone who needs guaranteed leads, not a prospecting tool

### FAQ

**What geography do you cover?**
We're starting with Austin, TX and surrounding areas. If you're in a different market, reach out — we're expanding based on demand.

**How is this different from raw permit data?**
Raw permit data is hundreds of rows of unfiltered records. PermitFeed filters for the project types relevant to your business, excludes low-value entries, removes noise, and sorts by priority. You get a short list worth acting on, not a spreadsheet to wade through.

**Is this exclusive to my company?**
We limit distribution — one operator per service type per territory. We won't send the same filtered list to a direct competitor in your area. The underlying data is public record, but the filtered version we build for your territory is limited.

**What does it cost?**
We're currently running pilot programs with operators in the Austin area. Reach out to discuss pricing for your territory.

Request a sample below. Tell us your service area and what kind of work you do — we'll build a sample list for your territory.
