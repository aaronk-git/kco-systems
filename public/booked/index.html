Frequently asked questions

How fast is the install?

About 14 business days from sign-up. We provision a Twilio number, build your custom voice agent prompt, integrate it with your calendar, and run final testing — then it's live.

Who's the AI? Will it sound like a robot?

Booked is built on best-in-class voice AI (ElevenLabs voices, Deepgram speech recognition). Most callers don't know it's an AI in the first 30 seconds. We disclose it on every call to stay compliant.

What does "qualified" mean?

You define it. We build the qualification script around YOUR criteria — budget thresholds, project types, geography, timeline, property requirements, anything. The AI uses your definition, not ours.

Will it work with my calendar / CRM?

Native integrations: Google Calendar, Cal.com, Housecall Pro, JobTread, Buildertrend. Other systems via webhook (we'll build it during install).

Is there a setup fee?

No. Just the monthly subscription. Install costs are baked into your first month.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. One click in your dashboard. No exit fees, no contracts, no penalty period.

What happens if I cancel?

Your AI line goes offline within 24 hours of cancellation. Your call history and transcripts are exported to you and then deleted on request.

Does it work for businesses besides hardscape?

Right now we focus on residential outdoor living and hardscape contractors ($1-10M revenue). We expand to adjacent verticals as we grow — book a call if you want to discuss your business.

What if I have technical questions during onboarding?

You get direct Slack/email access to AK throughout onboarding. Plus a weekly tuning call to refine the agent based on real call data.

What about the 75% refund?

Once your line goes live, you have 7 days to evaluate. If it's not a fit for any reason, request a refund — we send back 75% of your first month, no questions asked. We keep the rest to cover install costs. The 7-day clock starts the day your AI line is live, not the day you sign up — so you get a real evaluation window after the build is done.

Why only 3 new clients per month?

Booked is run solo — no outsourcing, no junior team. Limited roster keeps quality high and ensures every install gets full attention. Once full, waitlist only.
