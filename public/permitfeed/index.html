FAQ
What geography do you cover?
Austin, TX metro. Expanding based on demand — reach out if you're in a different market.
How is this different from raw permit data?
Raw feeds are hundreds of unfiltered rows. We filter for relevant project types, remove noise, and sort by priority. You get a short list worth acting on.
Is this exclusive to my company?
We limit distribution by territory and service type — we aim to avoid sending the same filtered list to direct competitors in your area. The underlying data is public record, but the filtered product we build for your territory is not shared broadly. Availability is subject to your market.
What does it cost?
We're running pilot programs in the Austin area now. Reach out to discuss pricing for your territory.
