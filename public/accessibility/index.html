Our Commitment

Karatas & Company LLC, doing business as KCo Systems, is committed to making our website and products accessible to all users. We aim to follow established web accessibility standards and continually improve the experience for everyone.

What We Do

  • Use semantic HTML and proper heading structure
  • Maintain sufficient color contrast ratios
  • Avoid using color alone as an indicator — we pair color with text labels and icons
  • Ensure interactive elements are keyboard-accessible
  • Use descriptive alt text for meaningful images
  • Design with readable font sizes and clear visual hierarchy

Feedback

If you encounter an accessibility barrier on our website or have suggestions for improvement, we want to hear from you. Please contact us at info@kco-systems.com and we will do our best to address the issue.
